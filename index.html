About Ocean Agri

Helping Farmers Grow More with Reliable Agri Solutions

Ocean Agri delivers high-performance agricultural protection products designed for real Indian farming conditions. From small farmers to large agribusinesses, we support growers with solutions that improve crop quality, reduce losses, and increase yield.

Our range includes mulching films, crop covers, netlon nets, weed mats, low tunnel systems, agri wires, crop support nets, and spray pumps — all built for durability, performance, and long-term use in the field.

  • UV-stabilized materials for longer life in harsh sunlight
  • Crop-specific solutions for different farming needs
  • Trusted by 10,000+ farmers across 20+ states
