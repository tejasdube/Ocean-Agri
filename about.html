Our Story

Helping Farmers Grow More with Reliable Agri Solutions

Ocean Agri delivers high-performance agricultural protection products designed for real Indian farming conditions. From small farmers to large agribusinesses, we support growers with solutions that improve crop quality, reduce losses, and increase yield.

Our range includes mulching films, crop covers, netlon nets, weed mats, low tunnel systems, agri wires, crop support nets, and spray pumps — all built for durability, performance, and long-term use in the field.

  • UV-stabilized materials for longer life in harsh sunlight
  • Crop-specific solutions for different farming needs
  • Trusted by 10,000+ farmers across 20+ states
Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted agricultural products brand — empowering every farmer with science-backed, affordable solutions that increase yield, reduce losses and protect crops from seedling to harvest.

Our Mission

To continuously innovate our product range to meet evolving agricultural challenges; to build a strong, accessible dealer network; and to educate farmers on how technology-driven agri products can transform their farm's productivity.

Our Product Philosophy

Built for the Indian Farm, Backed by Science

At Ocean Agri, we believe that good products should be accessible to every farmer — not just large agribusinesses. That's why our product range is designed to serve both small-scale farmers with modest land holdings and large commercial operations with extensive cultivation. We prioritize:

  • UV Stability — All films and covers are UV-stabilized to withstand India's intense sunlight
  • Crop Specificity — Different crops have different needs; our variants are tailored accordingly
  • Durability — Products built to last through full growing seasons and multiple cycles
  • Affordability — Competitive pricing to ensure every farmer can access quality protection
